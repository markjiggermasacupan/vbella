Barong Tagalog

Barong Tagalog is an embroidered formal men's wear in the Philippines. It is appropriately referred to as the Baro ng Tagalog (dress of tagalog).

Filipiniana

Traditional dress that can be identified by its puffy butterfly sleeves, plain blouse (baro or camisa), long skirt (saya), and a pañuelo (a square of cloth used as a head covering or worn as a scarf around the neck) worn over the shoulders.
